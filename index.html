オタクという文化について

Recently, "OTAKU" has also been used as a word to represent Japanese subculture. How do you understand the word "OTAKU"? Some people think of "OTAKU" as a culture, while others see it as a hobby. The word "OTAKU" is spreading, and it is dangerous to define "OTAKU" based on personal opinions and to broadcast that definition. This site summarizes otaku who prefer "RYOSANGATA" and "JIRAIKEI" fashion.
